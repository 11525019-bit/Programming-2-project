## Short description about the game
This game is a project for __programming method 2__ so the gameplay is _simple_ and you can play the game in _5-10 minutes_. In this game you will choose how to respond to the conversation of the characters, your choice will lead to _different endings_.

## About
The game is about an adult that is apathetic, his life is overwhelmed by the busy work and his co-workers. This game is a visual novel game and it has a cold and dark tone. The idea of the game is about the suspicion of the main character about the existence of the world and his own life.
## Fearures
The game has branching story, multiple endings. Moreover, the choices of the player will affect the plot and ending.
## Story
That night, Martin (the main character) woke up as his phone was ringing, the atmosphere was so silent and peaceful, but his mind was busy thinking about the stress caused by his work and co-workers. He went for a walk to release the pressure, forgetting that he had a date with a friend. He unintentionally met the girl that was waiting for him. Then they walked and chatted together. They got in a convenience store after that. Having bought their drinks, they sat down by the window and admired the view while talking. Although Martin was talking to Luna, he kept wondering the existence of the world, himself and Luna also. After a while, they moved to a near park and continue chatting, they talked about their existence, they wanted to if they are real people or they’re just something else. The game ends at that moment and leaves us the question whether we exist or just like the characters in the game. And the game gives us 4 endings based on how we chose to respond to Luna.
