## Short description about the game
This game is a project for __programming method 2__ so the gameplay is _simple_ and you can play the game in _5-10 minutes_. In this game you will choose how to respond to the conversation of the characters, your choice will lead to _different endings_.

## About
The game is about an adult that is apathetic, his life is overwhelmed by the busy work and his co-workers. This game is a visual novel game and it has a cold and dark tone. The idea of the game is about the suspicion of the main character about the existence of the world and his own life.
## Features
The game has branching story, multiple endings. Moreover, the choices of the player will affect the plot and ending.
## Introduction about the story
After a long day at work, you come home, collapse into bed, and drift off for what should have been a short nap. But when you wake up, it is already midnight. The apartment is quiet, the night is still, and somewhere outside, a girl is waiting for you.

In this story-driven game, you will step into the shoes of an ordinary office worker pulled into an unusual late-night journey through a convenience store, a silent street, and a lonely park. Every choice you make shapes not only the path ahead, but also the conversation that unfolds between you and the girl beside you.

As the night goes on, she begins to reveal “the truth.” What that truth means depends on how you respond, how you act, and how much you are willing to understand before the night ends.

A quiet, emotional experience about missed time, hidden feelings, and the choices that define what comes next.

Goodluck on exploring the invisible inside yourself.
